01 · Color Palette
What goes where.

On Forest (Dark)

  • Body text → Ivory #F5F1EB
  • Labels & eyebrows → Brass Soft
  • Accent elements → Brass
  • Tertiary / muted → Slate Soft

On Ivory (Light)

  • Body text → Ink #1C1C1C
  • Section eyebrows → Brass
  • Headings → Forest #1B4332
  • Supporting text → Slate

Forest — Use For

  • Wordmark, hero backgrounds
  • Business card face
  • Primary navigation surfaces
  • Never substitute off-brand greens

Brass — Use For

  • CTAs and call-to-action elements
  • Bottom bar of the triple mark
  • Title accents and emphasis
  • Brass earns its weight — use sparingly

04 · Brand Voice
Say this. Not that.
Write this
  • "A little tax-loss harvesting never hurt nobody."
  • "We measure success by how your wealth performs — and by what it stands for."
  • "Ten years of giving with purpose, and counting."
  • "Your portfolio can reflect your values. Performance is not the price."
Not this
  • "Leverage proprietary tax optimization solutions for HNW clients."
  • "MOR is committed to maximizing risk-adjusted stakeholder value."
  • "Q3 portfolio performance metrics are now available for review."
  • "SRI strategies may underperform conventional benchmarks (see disclosures)."
